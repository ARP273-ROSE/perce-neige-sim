Supplier & partner of elevator ropes, lifting equipments, architectural ropes and fuses

Welcome to BRUGG Lifting

At BRUGG Lifting, we combine tradition with innovation to provide advanced and reliable solutions in the fields of elevator ropes, lifting technology, and architectural ropes. As pioneers in the industry with over a century of experience, we strive to push the boundaries of what is possible and maximize the efficiency and safety of your projects.

About us

Innovative elevator solutions for modern buildings..

Our wide range of our elevator ropes, are specifically designed for safety and efficiency in vertical mobility. From hoist ropes, compensating ropes and special ropes with electrical conductors to our industry-first coated ropes and belts, our solutions help your projects reach new heights.

Our products

Elevator Traction Systems by BRUGG Lifting

Together with our partner LANCOR, we offer you a high-quality drive system perfectly matched to the coated ropes and belts from BRUGG Lifting.
Our products

Reliable lifting technology for demanding loads.

BRUGG Lifting offers a comprehensive range of lifting equipment, including wire ropes, textile slings, and special solutions for various applications in industry and construction. Our products guarantee the highest safety standards and efficiency to make your workday easier and your worksite safer.

Our products

Aesthetics and functionality in perfect harmony

Our architectural ropes open up new possibilities for creative and functional structures. Whether for railings, green facades, or other structural applications, BRUGG Lifting combines innovative technology with aesthetic design to bring your vision to life.

Comprehensive services from BRUGG Lifting.

We understand the importance of reliable and safe lifting technology for realizing architectural visions and demanding construction projects. Our wide range of services is designed to provide you not only with products but also with customized solutions and comprehensive support.
